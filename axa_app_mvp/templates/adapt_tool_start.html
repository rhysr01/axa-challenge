Home Safety Assessment

Our ADAPT tool helps you identify potential fall hazards in your home with a simple photo-based assessment. In just a few minutes, you'll receive personalized recommendations to make your living space safer.

How It Works

1 Take Photos

Capture images of key areas in your home, including the living room, bedroom, bathroom, and hallways.

2 Answer Questions

Provide some basic information about your mobility and health to help us personalize the assessment.

3 Get Results

Receive a detailed report with identified hazards, risk levels, and actionable recommendations.

Benefits of Completing This Assessment:

  • Identify potential fall hazards before they cause harm
  • Receive personalized recommendations based on your specific needs
  • Create a safer living environment for you or your loved ones
  • Peace of mind knowing your home is as safe as possible
