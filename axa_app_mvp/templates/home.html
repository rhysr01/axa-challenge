Home Safety Tips

Simple changes can make a big difference in preventing falls at home.

Lighting

  • • Keep walkways well-lit
  • • Use night lights in hallways and bathrooms
  • • Place lamps within easy reach of beds

Trip Hazards

  • • Remove loose rugs or use non-slip backing
  • • Keep walkways clear of clutter
  • • Secure loose cords and wires

Bathroom Safety

  • • Install grab bars in shower and near toilet
  • • Use non-slip mats in tub/shower
  • • Consider a raised toilet seat if needed
